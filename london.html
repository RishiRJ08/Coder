London — Quick Tourist Guide

Major attractions and an interactive map showing their locations.

Tower of London

Historic castle housing the Crown Jewels.

Buckingham Palace

The monarch's official London residence.

London Eye

Giant observation wheel on the South Bank.

British Museum

World-class collections of art and antiquities.

Tower Bridge

Iconic bascule bridge across the Thames.

Big Ben & Palace of Westminster

Famous clock tower and the UK Parliament buildings.

Tate Modern

Modern art museum on the South Bank.

St Paul's Cathedral

Christopher Wren's masterpiece with a famous dome.

Hyde Park

Large central park: boating, walking, and concerts.
